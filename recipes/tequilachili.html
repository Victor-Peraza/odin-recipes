Tequila Chili

This simple and quick one-pot chili has great flavor and is nice and meaty.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can italian-style stew tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can light red kidney beans, drained
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 fluid ounces tequila
  • 1 table spoon chili powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Recipe Instructions

  1. Brown the ground beef in a large pot over medium heat
  2. breaking it up with a spoon while it cooks; drain
  3. Stir in the tomatoes, kidney beans, onion, garlic, tomato sauce, tequila, chili powder, cumin, salt, and cayenne pepper
  4. simmer 20 minutes

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving:379 calories; protein 27g; carbohydrates 30.2g; fat 14.4g; cholesterol 69mg; sodium 1405.5mg.
